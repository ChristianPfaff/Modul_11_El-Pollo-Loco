How to play Ell Pollo Logo

Short Description

At the beginning Peppe will greet you. Click the start button and the game will start. The chickens are the enemies which can hurt you. Everytime when you get a hit by the chicken you will lost live-energy (look at heart-bar) on the top. Jump up at the money to reload your live-energy. You can shoot the chicken or skip one. Once you habe no bottles left you can reload them by sample bottles on your way. Go further to the right and you will encounter the endboss. Shot him and you are the winner!!
